# 📜 Changelog

All notable changes to the **Job Tracking System** will be documented in this file.

---

## 🚀 [2.0.0] - 2026-04-27

### ✨ Added
- **Multi-Channel Support**: Integrated WhatsApp, Email, and Manual review channels.
- **OCR Engine**: Automated text extraction from job postings in image formats (JPG, PNG, PDF).
- **Email Integration**: Full SMTP support with customizable subject and body templates.
- **WhatsApp Webhooks**: Real-time message reception via WhatsApp Business API.
- **Enhanced Filtering**: New endpoints to filter jobs by communication channel.
- **Docker Excellence**: Full multi-container orchestration for seamless deployment.
- **Modern UI**: Completely revamped frontend with Tailwind CSS and Angular 19.
- **Documentation Hub**: Comprehensive guides and technical references in `docs/`.

### 🔄 Changed
- **API Versioning**: Upgraded all endpoints to `v2` (e.g., `/api/v2/jobs`).
- **Template Schema**: Extended models to support separate email subject and body templates.
- **Frontend Core**: Migrated to Angular 19 for improved performance and features.

### 🛠️ Fixed
- **CORS Policies**: Optimized for production environments.
- **Database Migrations**: Resolved ordering and dependency issues.

---

## 🏁 [1.0.0] - 2025-01-01

### ✨ Added
- **Initial Release**: Core job tracking and classification engine.
- **Smart Classification**: Auto-detection of Backend, Frontend, and Fullstack roles.
- **Data Extraction**: Automated phone number and job title parsing.
- **Template CRUD**: Full management of response templates.
- **Angular Dashboard**: Initial job management interface.
- **Clean Architecture**: Robust .NET 8 backend foundation.
